# First-500 enrichment started — 2026-07-21

The user authorized immediate creation of complete information for the first 500
words in the uploaded frequency dictionary, including main sentences, extra
sentences, translations, and forms.

## Scale decision

The dataset will not be produced as 500 opaque one-shot prose responses. Each
entry will be a resumable structured record with morphology, sense, multiple
original sentence candidates, one selected main sentence, optional teaching
examples, translations, quality flags, and approval/locking fields. Generation
and validation will operate in bounded batches so tens of thousands of sentence
candidates remain editable and auditable.

## Source finding

The PDF is *A Frequency Dictionary of German*, second edition, Routledge, 2020,
by Erwin Tschirner and Jupp Möhring. Its copyright page states all rights
reserved. Therefore the private rank signal may guide analysis, but book
definitions and example sentences will not be reproduced in public canonical
data. All deck sentences must be independently authored.

## Extraction status

The source has 315 PDF pages and a text layer. A broad scan for the ranked-list
boundary hung and was aborted without modifying the PDF. The next approach is
targeted page probing with strict time limits, followed by a deterministic parser
once the page layout is known.
