Early public preview

A German vocabulary deck built for context, not isolated words.

This is an independently authored, AI-assisted experiment toward a roughly 6,000-card German Anki course for English speakers. The first 200 frequency-guided cards are ready for public review.

What I would love feedback on

  • Are the German sentences natural and idiomatic?
  • Are the English translations and grammar notes accurate?
  • Is each card useful without becoming overwhelming?
  • Would sentence and word audio materially improve this format?
  • What would make this worth using alongside an existing course?

Audio has not been added yet. The plan is to test native-quality or high-quality TTS audio on a small sample before applying it across the deck. Frequency position is retained as internal curriculum metadata and is not shown on the learner-facing card.
